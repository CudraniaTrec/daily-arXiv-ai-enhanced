### [1] [The Bottleneck Birthday Problem](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00101)
*Chijul B. Tripathy*

Main category: cs.DM

TL;DR: 本文提出并研究了瓶颈生日问题，创新性地用组合数学方法求解，给出精确递推公式与数值结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典生日问题研究了多少人中有两人同一天生日的概率。本文关注于研究多至r个人同一天生日的极限人数，即在每一天生日人数最多不超过r的情况下，最多可以有多少人，使这一事件发生的概率至少为1/2。

Method: 采用调研与引用占据问题和负载均衡领域的分析技巧，推导了精确计算概率和人数的递推公式。此外，创新性地应用限制性第二类Stirling数建立了新的递推关系。

Result: 成功推导了概率和人数的递推公式，并利用这些递推公式进行了数值计算，展示了结果的可行性和准确性。

Conclusion: 本文提出了瓶颈生日问题（BBP），并给出了基于组合数学（如Stirling数）的递推方法来精确计算答案，最终通过实现递推公式给出数值结果。

Abstract: We introduce a fun problem that can be considered as a variant of the classic birthday problem, the Bottleneck Birthday Problem (BBP). It is stated as: what is the maximum number of people we have to choose so that no day of the year has more than $r \geq 1$ birthdays incident on it with probability at least 1/2? We provide a survey of techniques used in the literature on occupancy and load balancing problems to derive recurrence relations for exact computation of the probability and the number of people, keeping probability fixed at a threshold. Further, we show that restricted Stirling numbers of the second kind can be used to derive an additional recurrence, in a novel way. We provide numerical results from an implementation of the recurrences.

### [2] [Structural and Spectral Properties of Strictly Interval Graphs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01175)
*Lilian Markenzon,Claudia Justel*

Main category: cs.DM

TL;DR: 作者提出了严格区间图的新刻画，设计了线性识别算法，定义了SI-core图并分析了其拉普拉斯谱积分性，为图论中的结构与谱性质研究提供了新进展。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究弦图的一个特殊子类，严格区间图，以加深对其结构和性质的理解，同时推动图论在相关领域的应用。

Method: 提出并刻画严格区间图这种图的特性，进而设计出一种线性时间识别算法。此外，定义了严格区间图的一个新子类SI-core图，并分析其结构以及谱积分性（拉普拉斯积分性）。

Result: 发现了一种严格区间图的简单刻画方法，并据此给出了线性时间识别算法。进一步提出了SI-core图的定义，证明其为非分裂图且非协图，并展示其谱积分性。

Conclusion: 本文不仅拓展了图论中弦图与区间图的研究，还提出了更高效的识别算法，同时发现了SI-core图的谱积分性，为图谱理论提供了新的实例和应用方向。

Abstract: In this paper we deal with a subclass of chordal graphs, which are simultaneously strictly chordal and interval, the strictly interval graphs. We present a new characterization of the class that leads to a simple linear recognition algorithm. Next we introduce a new subclass of strictly interval graphs, the $SI$-core graphs, that are non-split and non-cograph graphs and show that several elements of the new class are Laplacian integral.

### [3] [Heuristic algorithms for the stochastic critical node detection problem](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01497)
*Tuguldur Bayarsaikhan,Altannar Chinchuluun,Ashwin Arulselvan,Panos Pardalos*

Main category: cs.DM

TL;DR: 本文针对现实网络中边存在不确定性的关键节点检测问题，提出了高效的启发式和学习方法，并在不同实验条件下验证了其高性能和可扩展性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在实际生活中，很多系统可以用图来建模，为了评估网络的脆弱性，需要找到关键节点，其移除可显著影响网络连通性。已有的问题版本多为确定性，但现实中边的存在常具有不确定性。因此，本文考虑了边存在概率的不确定性（随机性），以更好适配实际应用情况。

Method: 本文提出了基于启发式和基于学习的方法用于求解随机关键节点检测问题，并与现有算法进行比较。方法在不同规模和密度的随机图上进行实验，并对边存在概率取自不同分布的情况展开评估。

Result: 实验结果表明，启发式算法具有较强的性能和高可扩展性；而基于学习的方法推理时间几乎随网络规模和密度变化保持稳定。两种方法在效果方面均表现优异。

Conclusion: 提出的启发式和学习方法能够有效解决带边存在概率的关键节点检测问题，具有良好的性能和可扩展性。实验验证了新方法在不同参数分布和大量随机图上的有效性。

Abstract: Given a network, the critical node detection problem finds a subset of nodes whose removal disrupts the network connectivity. Since many real-world systems are naturally modeled as graphs, assessing the vulnerability of the network is essential, with applications in transportation systems, traffic forecasting, epidemic control, and biological networks. In this paper, we consider a stochastic version of the critical node detection problem, where the existence of edges is given by certain probabilities. We propose heuristics and learning-based methods for the problem and compare them with existing algorithms. Experimental results performed on random graphs from small to larger scales, with edge-survival probabilities drawn from different distributions, demonstrate the effectiveness of the methods. Heuristic methods often illustrate the strongest results with high scalability, while learning-based methods maintain nearly constant inference time as the network size and density grow.

### [4] [Unconditional Time and Space Complexity Lower Bounds for Intersection Non-Emptiness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00297)
*Michael Wehar*

Main category: cs.FL

TL;DR: 本论文强化和扩展了DFA交集非空性问题的下界，首次给出无条件多项式级别下界，并分析其对PTIME、PSPACE等复杂性类的理论影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 本文旨在重新审视确定性有限自动机（DFA）交集非空性问题的已知下界，尤其关注其计算复杂性理论基础。通过分析该问题的难度，可以推动理论计算机科学中复杂性类之间关系的进一步理解。

Method: 作者首先强化了Kasai和Iwata（1985）通过归约给出的条件性时间复杂性下界；随后结合Williams（2025）在空间高效模拟确定性时间的新进展，给出了无条件的时间复杂性下界；最后分析了该问题对复杂性类关系（如PTIME, DSPACE, PSPACE, EXPTIME）的潜在影响。

Result: 文中得到该问题的无条件时间复杂性下界为Ω(n^2/ log^3(n) loglog^2(n))，且在固定DFA数量下考虑复杂性时推导出若其难于某固定多项式类，则PTIME ⊆ DSPACE(n^c)，且PSPACE = EXPTIME等重要复杂性理论结果成立。

Conclusion: DFA交集非空性问题的下界既有新的无条件证明，其理论影响也关系到重要复杂性类的关系。文中工作推动了对交集问题复杂性的理解，提出了更强的复杂性假设归约。

Abstract: We reinvestigate known lower bounds for the Intersection Non-Emptiness Problem for Deterministic Finite Automata (DFA's). We first strengthen conditional time complexity lower bounds from T. Kasai and S. Iwata (1985) which showed that Intersection Non-Emptiness is not solvable more efficiently unless there exist more efficient algorithms for non-deterministic logarithmic space ($\texttt{NL}$). Next, we apply a recent breakthrough from R. Williams (2025) on the space efficient simulation of deterministic time to show an unconditional $Ω(\frac{n^2}{\log^3(n) \log\log^2(n)})$ time complexity lower bound for Intersection Non-Emptiness. Finally, we consider implications that would follow if Intersection Non-Emptiness for a fixed number of DFA's is computationally hard for a fixed polynomial time complexity class. These implications include $\texttt{PTIME} \subseteq \texttt{DSPACE}(n^c)$ for some $c \in \mathbb{N}$ and $\texttt{PSPACE} = \texttt{EXPTIME}$.

</details>
